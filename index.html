INTRODUCING THE ELECTRONICA "ЭИМ-4" HANDHELD GAMING SYSTEM!

THE FUTURE OF PORTABLE PLAY HAS HATCHED!

Tired of bulky, boring game watches? Say goodbye to flickery LCDs and hello to real video game action—anywhere you go!
The "ЭИМ-4" Electronica’s boldest innovation yet: a sleek, egg-shaped handheld packed with full-color graphics, crisp sound, and addictive gameplay that fits right in your jacket pocket!

“It’s like carrying a mini arcade in your backpack!”
— Tech Toy Monthly, October 1987

  • Vibrant 2.5” Full-Color Screen – Finally, portable games in red, blue, green, and yellow! (No more gray blobs!)
  • Ergonomic design – Fits perfectly in your hands for hours of play!
  • Built-In Stereo Speaker – Hear every beep, boop, and explosion in glorious mono (stereo headphones sold separately)!
  • Auto Power-Saver – Game pauses when you stop playing! (Batteries not included—but uses standard AA!)
  • Real D-Pad + Action Buttons – Just like your NES at home!
  • Display: 160 × 144 pixel full-color LCD
  • Sound: 3-channel PSG (Programmable Sound Generator)
  • Power: 2 × AA batteries (up to 8 hours play!)
  • Dimensions: 4.2” × 2.8” × 1.1”
  • Weight: Just 6.4 oz!
  • Cartridge port: 24-pin secure lock
